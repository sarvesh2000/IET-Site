The Institution of Engineering and Technology

IET

The institution of Engineering and Technology is one of the oldest multidisciplinary professional bodies based in UK and it has a worldwide membership of 167,000. The motto of IET is “WORKING TO ENGINEER A BETTER WORLD” .IT inspires a wider engineering community and influencing government and standards to advance.

IET CHENNAI LOCAL NETWORK (CLN)

The IET CLN is the largest network in South Asia and outside the UK with over 6000 members . The CLN’s strength lies with high student memberships who volunteer many events within and outside local network. The IET CLN comprises of 9 academic affiliates and 15 on campus groups spread over Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh.

INDUSTRY INSTITUTION SUMMIT

The Industries and Institutions which have been operating as separate entities for long ,are rapidly moving closer to each other to create opportunities for skill development ,innovation and technology transfer.
